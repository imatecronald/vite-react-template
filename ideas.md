# Imatec Website Design Ideas

## Approach 1: "Rural Professional" — Grounded Authority
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** New Zealand Vernacular Modernism — honest materials, practical beauty, no pretension.

**Core Principles:**
- Trust through clarity: every element earns its place
- Warm professionalism: approachable but credible
- Local identity: NZ rural colour and texture woven into a modern grid

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy blue (#1B2E5E) as the primary anchor — stable, trustworthy, corporate without being cold. Lime green (#7DC800) as the brand accent, echoing the existing Imatec identity and NZ farmland. Warm off-white (#F7F5F0) for backgrounds to avoid clinical sterility. Charcoal (#2D2D2D) for body text.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric split-column hero with image occupying 60% right, text left-anchored. Services in a staggered card grid. Testimonials in a horizontal scroll band. Sticky nav with phone number always visible.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin lime-green left-border accent on section headings
- Diagonal section dividers (subtle clip-path) between hero and content
- Circular photo frames for team/testimonial portraits

**Interaction Philosophy:** Confident and direct — hover states reveal lime-green underlines, CTAs pulse subtly on load, scroll-triggered fade-ins for content sections.

**Animation:** Subtle entrance animations (fade-up, 0.3s ease) on scroll. No excessive motion. Phone CTA button has a gentle pulse ring.

**Typography System:** Headings in "Barlow Condensed" Bold (strong, compact, NZ-practical feel). Body in "Source Sans 3" Regular (highly readable). Accent labels in "Barlow" SemiBold uppercase with letter-spacing.
</text>

## Approach 2: "Clean Tech Confidence" — Modern Local Expert
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Contemporary B2B SaaS meets local service business — clean, structured, conversion-focused.

**Core Principles:**
- Conversion first: every section has a clear next action
- Structured hierarchy: information flows naturally from problem to solution
- Restrained colour: navy + green used sparingly for maximum impact

**Color Philosophy:** White (#FFFFFF) dominant background for cleanliness. Navy (#1A3A6B) for headers and nav. Lime green (#6DBE00) for all CTAs and highlights. Light grey (#F4F6F8) for alternating section backgrounds.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with overlay text. Below-fold sections alternate left/right image-text pairs. Stats bar (years in business, clients served, locations). Testimonial carousel. Two-column contact section.

**Signature Elements:**
- Green pill badges on service cards ("Most Popular", "For Farmers")
- Stats counter animation on scroll
- Sticky top bar with phone numbers for both locations

**Interaction Philosophy:** Functional and fast — users should be able to find what they need in 2 clicks. Hover cards expand with service detail.

**Animation:** Counter animations, smooth scroll, card hover lift (translateY -4px, shadow increase).

**Typography System:** "DM Sans" for headings (modern, geometric, friendly). "DM Sans" Regular for body. Clean and consistent.
</text>

## Approach 3: "Trusted Local" — Warm Community Expert
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Community-first service brand — warm, human, deeply local. Think a trusted tradesperson, not a tech corporation.

**Core Principles:**
- Human over technical: faces, names, real stories
- Community identity: Matamata and Tauranga are characters in the story
- Simplicity signals confidence: if it's clear, it's credible

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#1B3A6B) and lime green (#7DC800) as primary brand colours. Warm cream (#FBF8F3) for section backgrounds. Earthy mid-grey (#6B7280) for secondary text. Green used for all positive signals (checkmarks, CTAs, badges).

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with NZ landscape photo and bold headline. Services as icon-led horizontal strips. "Why Imatec" as a bold statement section with 4 pillars. Testimonials woven between service sections (not isolated). Location cards side by side at the bottom.

**Signature Elements:**
- Hand-drawn-style underline on key phrases (CSS SVG path)
- Location pin icons in lime green for Matamata/Tauranga
- "As seen in" trust bar with client logos (Daltons, Matamata Medical, etc.)

**Interaction Philosophy:** Warm and unhurried — hover states are gentle colour shifts, not abrupt. The site feels like talking to a local expert, not filling out a corporate form.

**Animation:** Gentle fade-ins. Testimonial quotes slide in from left. Section headings animate letter by letter on first scroll.

**Typography System:** "Nunito" ExtraBold for display headings (rounded, friendly, approachable). "Nunito" Regular for body. Feels welcoming without being childish.
</text>

## Selected Approach: Approach 1 — "Rural Professional"

This approach best balances Imatec's need to rebuild trust with existing clients, appeal to rural NZ small business owners and farmers, and project a fresh, professional image without abandoning the existing brand identity. The Barlow Condensed typography gives it a strong, no-nonsense character that resonates with practical Kiwi business owners, while the lime green accent maintains brand continuity.
